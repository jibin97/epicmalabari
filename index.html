About Us

Epic Malabari limited is a wellness-driven initiative committed to creating safe and inspiring spaces where people can pause, reflect, and reconnect with themselves. Our flagship program includes a one-day summer camps, walk to nature, explore the world.


It is thoughtfully designed to restore mental clarity, encourage emotional release, inspire creative expression and promote deep relaxation all through the transformative power of nature, storytelling, art, and movement.

Why Epic Malabari?

No Meds. No Masks. Just You. We offer holistic wellness without dependency on substances.

Nature as a healer scientifically and spiritually, nature has profound calming effects. We use it as our core environment.

Inclusive & Supportive every session is designed to be safe, non-judgmental, and deeply supportive.

Led by experts our team includes trained facilitators in therapeutic art, movement, and narrative healing.
